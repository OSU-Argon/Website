      ## Publications
      
      Since 1977 investigators, students and post-docs in our laboratory have
      produced 40Ar/39Ar age data that appeared in more than [195 peer-reviewed
      publications](/publications/#recent) and in [31 Senior, MSc and PhD
      theses](/publications/#theses). This long list of papers could not have
      been possible without the more than 100 visitors over the last decades and
      our numerous national and international collaborators.

      Below you will find a list of all our publications with the **names** of
      the principal investigators, students and post-docs of this laboratory
      underlined.
